INSTRUCTIONS

  1. The main objective of this game is to help Little Bo Peep with her small problem- that is, she seemed to have lost her sheep.

  2. Unfortunately, this game is not as simple as just finding lost sheep.

  3. One of the sheep is actually a wolf in sheep's clothing, and to win the game, you have to successfully bring all the lost sheep to Little Bo Peep without bringing the wolf as well.

  4. Clues are given every time you pick the correct sheep.

  5. To bring the sheep back to Little Bo Peep, simply click on it.
