  • Game Inspiration
    • “Find the sheep” is a game inspired by the nursery rhyme “Little Bo Peep Has Lost Her Sheep,” where a girl named Little Bo Peep has lost her sheep. However, instead of waiting for the sheep to come home like in the nursery rhyme, the player of the game will instead help her find her sheep.
    • However, there is a twist as one of the sheep is actually a wolf. If the player selects the wolf, they loose.
